SciTech Week 2019

       Bulacan State University (BulSU), celebrate it's Science and Technology Week 2019 – “Science for the People: Enabling Technologies for Sustainable Development”, hosted by the College of Industrial Technology (CIT), together with College of Information and Communications Technology (CICT), College of Science (CS), and College of Engineering (COE) during September 23-27, 2019. The event is a week-long festivity with a series of activities such as seminars, competitions, and exhibits headed by the Dean of the College of Industrial Technology Dr. Keno Piad.



       The four colleges are represented with different elemental flags that represents their respective colleges. College of Industrial Technology (CIT) represented with a water elemental flag. College of Engineering (COE) represented with the fire elemental flag. College of Information and Communications Technology (CICT) represented by the wind elemental flag. College of Science (CS) represented by the water elemental flag. Courtesy of BulSU Science and Technology Week 2019 Official Page.



       Before the events started. Students from the four colleges gathered to march for the parade to welcome the incoming celebration of the Science and Technology Week. The parade start from the BulSU Activity Center to KB Gym and to officially announce the start of the SciTech Week 2019. Courtesy of BulSU Science and Technology Week 2019 Official Page.





Events

       The day started with a parade attended by the College of Engineering, College of Industrial Technology, College of Information and Communications Technology, and the College of Science and their respective Ambassadors, Techno Mascots, and Techno Minions.

       The parade proceeded to the KB Gym for the week's opening ceremony at 8 AM.

       "Since 2016, the BulSU started to observe SciTech Week as a meaningful gesture of cooperation with the DOST to put across the academic community and beyond, the relevance and impact of science and improve for the good of humanity. Indeed, science for the people.", said University President Dr. Cecilia Gascon during her speech during the opening ceremony.

       September 23, 2019 - In line with the celebration for the opening day of Science and Technology Week 2019.
